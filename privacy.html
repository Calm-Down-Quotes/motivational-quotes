Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Thank you for using Calm Down Quotes. Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit this website.

1. Information We Collect

We aim to keep data collection minimal.

  • Automatically collected (non-personal) data: basic technical information such as your browser type, device type, general usage patterns, and pages visited. This is collected via analytics tools and does not identify you personally.
  • Information you choose to provide: if you subscribe to a daily or periodic email list, we collect the email address you submit, and any additional information you voluntarily provide (for example, if you reply to an email).

2. Cookies & Analytics

This website uses analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how people use the site so we can improve performance and experience.

  • Analytics tools may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic, usage patterns, and basic technical data.
  • We do not use analytics data to personally identify you.
  • Data is viewed in aggregate form (for example, number of visits, popular pages, general device types).

You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings. The site will still function, but analytics may be less accurate.

3. Email Subscriptions

You may choose to join an email list to receive daily quotes or occasional updates.

  • When you subscribe, we store the email address you provide for the purpose of sending you quote emails and related updates.
  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in each email, or via any contact method listed on the website (if available).
  • Your email address is not sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes.
  • If we use a reputable email service provider to send emails, your address will be stored securely with that provider solely for delivering these messages.

4. How We Use Information

Information we collect is used to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the website.
  • Understand which pages and features are most helpful.
  • Send you emails you have explicitly subscribed to (for example, daily quotes or updates).
  • Maintain security, prevent misuse, and monitor for errors.

5. Third-Party Links

The website may contain links to external pages or services. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of third-party websites. You should review the privacy policies of those sites separately.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no website or online service can guarantee complete security. Use of this site and any email subscription is at your own discretion and risk.

7. Your Choices

  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings.
  • You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in each message.
  • You may choose not to provide an email address or any optional information.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version. Continued use of the website after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please refer to any contact details provided on the website (if available) or any contact options included in subscription emails.
